# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** May 9, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how **prodethan** ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on **prodethan.com** (the "Site"). By continuing to browse our Site, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

---

### 1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?
Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the owners of the site.

### 2. TYPES OF COOKIES WE USE

We use the following categories of cookies:

*   **Strictly Necessary Cookies:** These are essential for the operation of our Site. They include cookies that enable you to log into secure areas, use a shopping cart, or access "Guest" session features. Without these cookies, the services you have asked for (like digital downloads) cannot be provided.
*   **Functionality Cookies:** These are used to recognize you when you return to our Site. This enables us to personalize our content for you and remember your preferences.
*   **Third-Party Cookies (Stripe):** Because we use Stripe for payment processing, Stripe may place cookies on your device to ensure the security of transactions, prevent fraud, and handle the payment flow. These are governed by [Stripe's Cookie Policy](https://stripe.com/cookies-policy/legal).

### 3. HOW WE USE COOKIES
Specifically, we use cookies and local storage to:
*   **Manage Guest Sessions:** Link your anonymous browser session to your shopping cart and purchased digital downloads.
*   **User Preferences:** Remember your specific UI settings, such as your choice between **Light Mode** and **Dark Mode**, so your preferred theme is applied automatically on every visit.
*   **Security:** Verify your identity during the checkout process and prevent fraudulent activity.
*   **Session Continuity:** Ensure that your data remains synced across the Site during a single browsing session.

### 4. COOKIES AND YOUR "GUEST" ACCOUNT
**Critical Warning:** Because we allow anonymous purchases, your access to purchased files is often linked to your browser’s local storage or session cookies. If you clear your browser cookies or use "Incognito/Private" mode, you may lose access to your download area and your UI preferences (like Dark Mode) will be reset. In the event of lost access to purchases, you will need to contact us with proof of purchase to regain access.

### 5. CONTROLLING COOKIES
Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to block all cookies or to alert you when a cookie is being sent.

**Note:** If you use your browser settings to block all cookies (including strictly necessary cookies), you may not be able to access all or parts of our Site, and your ability to purchase or download beats will be compromised.
